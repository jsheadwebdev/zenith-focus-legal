Privacy Policy for Zenith Focus

Zenith Focus is a local-first Android focus timer and task tracker. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and what third-party services may apply when you use the app.

Effective date: April 21, 2026 Platform: Android Local-first app

1. Overview

Zenith Focus is designed to work primarily on your device. The app lets you manage focus sessions and tasks locally, without requiring an account or cloud sync.

The app itself is intended to store your task and session-related information on your device. The app also displays ads through Google AdMob, which may collect information as described in Google’s own privacy documentation.

2. Information the App Stores Locally

Zenith Focus stores certain information on your device so the app can function properly and preserve your progress between sessions.

  • Task titles and optional task details
  • Task completion state
  • The currently active task selection
  • Timer duration preferences
  • Completed focus session count
  • Onboarding and walkthrough state

This information is stored locally on your device and is used to make the app function as expected.

3. Information the App Does Not Require Directly

Zenith Focus does not currently require you to create an account.

Zenith Focus does not currently provide cloud sync.

Zenith Focus does not currently require direct sign-in, username registration, or password creation to use its main features.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Zenith Focus uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob and its related services may collect, process, and use information in accordance with Google’s own privacy policies and applicable legal requirements.

This may include information such as device identifiers, IP address, app interactions, diagnostics, approximate location derived from IP, and other information used for ad delivery, fraud prevention, measurement, or personalization depending on platform behavior, region, settings, and applicable law.

5. Third-Party Policy Reference

Because ads are provided through Google AdMob, Google may process data independently under its own privacy terms.

You should review Google’s privacy information and AdMob-related disclosures to understand how Google handles data in connection with advertising services.

6. How Information Is Used

Information handled by Zenith Focus is used to:

  • Store and display your tasks
  • Remember your selected active task
  • Preserve timer preferences and app state
  • Track completed focus sessions locally
  • Display advertising through AdMob
  • Maintain app stability and expected functionality

7. Data Sharing

The app’s locally stored productivity data is intended to remain on your device unless you choose to back up, export, or otherwise share it through your own device or platform tools.

However, third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob may receive and process data as part of serving ads and related services.

8. Data Retention

Locally stored app data remains on your device until you remove it, clear app storage, uninstall the app, or overwrite the data through normal app usage.

Third-party data retention, if any, is governed by the applicable third-party provider’s own policies.

9. Security

Reasonable efforts are made to keep the app stable and to limit data handling to what is needed for the app’s current functionality. However, no method of storage, transmission, or electronic processing can be guaranteed as completely secure.

10. Children’s Privacy

Zenith Focus is not directed specifically to children under the age required by applicable law in their jurisdiction. If you believe a child has provided personal information in a way that creates a legal concern, contact the developer using the contact details below.

11. Your Choices

You may be able to limit certain ad-related data use through:

  • Your device privacy settings
  • Your Google account ad settings
  • Regional consent tools or platform-level privacy controls, where applicable

You may also remove locally stored app data by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the app.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the app, legal requirements, or third-party service usage. Updates will be reflected on this page by revising the effective date.
